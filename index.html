The "Black Box" Problem

Why we are flying blind on iOS & how to fix it.

Current State: The Data Gap

Since Apple's privacy updates (iOS 14.5+), we cannot see where iPhone users come from. While Android is clear, iOS is a "Black Box."

Google / Android

100% Tracked

We use Device IDs. We know if ads or emails drive installs.

Apple iOS

0% Attribution

Device IDs are blocked. All installs look like "Organic" traffic.

The Journey: Where we lose them

1. The Outreach

We send an email or run an ad for "Kick It". The user is interested.

2. The Click

They click the link.
Without a tool, the trail ends here for iOS.

3. The "Black Box"

They go to the Apple App Store. We lose the connection between the Email and the Store.

4. The Solution

Branch / AppsFlyer bridges this gap by "fingerprinting" the click and matching it to the install.
